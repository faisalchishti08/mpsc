Political Developments (2022–2024)

  • June 2022: Eknath Shinde led a rebel faction of Shiv Sena MLAs, causing fall of MVA (Maha Vikas Aghadi) government (Uddhav Thackeray resigned). Shinde became CM with BJP's Devendra Fadnavis as Deputy CM.
  • 2023: Election Commission recognised Eknath Shinde faction as the official Shiv Sena (with bow-and-arrow symbol). Supreme Court issued complex ruling on anti-defection (Nabam Rebia judgment applied).
  • November 2024 elections: Mahayuti (Shinde Sena + BJP + NCP-Ajit Pawar) won with strong majority (~230/288 seats). Devendra Fadnavis became CM for the third time (sworn in Dec 2024).

Renaming of Aurangabad and Osmanabad

  • Aurangabad → Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar (official rename July 2023).
  • Osmanabad → Dharashiv (official rename July 2023).

Key Appointments and Awards

  • Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw, Ratan Tata (died Oct 2024) — prominent personalities linked to Maharashtra business.
  • Jasprit Bumrah — awarded Arjuna in cricket; plays for Mumbai in domestic cricket.

Development Boards and Regional Issues

  • Vidarbha, Marathwada, North Maharashtra development boards reconstituted/funded (farmer distress, drought-prone region focus).
  • Water disputes: Krishna–Koyna-Bhima rivers allocation with Karnataka, Andhra; Narmada project shares with Gujarat (Hatnur Dam, Tapi basin).
  • Gadchiroli LFZ (Left Wing Extremism): Ongoing operations; CRPF and state police coordination; Gadchiroli recently saw increase in investment post-clearances (steel plants, forest-land).

Maharashtra Connection

All the above are directly Maharashtra-specific. The Mahayuti political developments, Mukhyamantri Majhi Ladki Bahin Yojana (₹1,500/month), and Atal Setu (India's longest sea bridge) are the highest-priority current affairs items for 2024–25 MPSC cycle.

Key Facts

  • Samruddhi Mahamarg: Nagpur–Mumbai, ~701 km; Phase 1 (Dec 2022).
  • Atal Setu: 22 km, Sewri–Nhava Sheva; India's longest sea bridge (Jan 2024).
  • Mumbai Metro Line 3 (Aqua Line): Maharashtra's first underground metro (2024).
  • Aurangabad → Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar and Osmanabad → Dharashiv (July 2023).
  • Devendra Fadnavis: CM from Dec 2024 (3rd term); Mahayuti alliance.
  • Ladki Bahin Yojana: ₹1,500/month for women 21–65; launched Aug 2024.
  • Namo Shetkari: State adds ₹6,000 to PM-KISAN → total ₹12,000/year for MH farmers.

Exam Traps

  • Atal Setu is a sea bridge (Sewri–Nhava Sheva), NOT a river bridge — don't confuse with other "longest bridge" claims.
  • Renaming happened July 2023, not before — earlier MPSC papers still used old names.
  • Eknath Shinde's faction (not Uddhav Thackeray's) is the officially recognised Shiv Sena — symbol and name both awarded by ECI.
  • Devendra Fadnavis was Deputy CM (2022–24) then CM again (Dec 2024) — don't say he was always CM.
