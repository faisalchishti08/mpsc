Ports — JNPT, Mumbai; airports PREMAINSINTMH

Overview

Maharashtra has India's most important port infrastructure — Jawaharlal Nehru Port (JNPT) at Navi Mumbai handles ~50–55% of India's container traffic, while Mumbai Port Trust (MbPT) manages general cargo and the Mumbai harbour. The state also has several minor ports along the 720-km Konkan coastline. Aviation is centred on Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (CSMIA), India's 2nd busiest airport. MPSC tests port types, capacity, major airports, and the distinction between major and minor ports.

JNPT — Deep Details

  • Location: Uran Taluka, Raigad district (Navi Mumbai across the harbour).
  • Total cargo handled (2022-23): ~6.5–7 million TEUs (Twenty-Foot Equivalent Units — container measure).
  • Nhava Sheva: Historical name for the area where JNPT is located; still colloquially used.
  • Connectivity: Dedicated Freight Corridor (DFC) — Western DFC connects JNPT to Delhi/Punjab hinterland for rail freight.
  • Mumbai Trans Harbour Link (MTHL / Atal Setu): 21.8 km sea bridge (India's longest); opened January 2024; connects Sewri (Mumbai) to Nhava Sheva (Navi Mumbai); reduces JNPT access time significantly.
  • JNPA is a statutory body under Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways (Government of India).

Maharashtra Maritime Board (MMB)

  • Statutory body under Maharashtra Maritime Board Act 1996.
  • Regulates and develops minor ports in Maharashtra.
  • HQ: Mumbai.
  • Promotes coastal shipping, fisheries jetties, and minor port development.
  • Key projects: Dighi Port expansion (PPP), Vadhavan Port (proposed new major port near Dahanu, Palghar — Central Govt project to reduce JNPT congestion).

Vadhavan Port — Proposed

  • Proposed new major port at Vadhavan, Palghar district.
  • Planned capacity: ~23 million TEUs eventually (larger than JNPT).
  • Developer: Sagarmala Programme (Ministry of Ports); Vadhavan Port Project under JNPA.
  • Key rationale: JNPT approaching capacity; Vadhavan has deeper natural draught; closer to mainline shipping lanes.

Sagarmala Programme — Maharashtra Components

  • Sagarmala Programme (Ministry of Ports, GoI): Modernisation of ports + port-led development + coastal shipping.
  • Maharashtra components: Vadhavan new port, JNPT expansion, coastal economic zones along Konkan, fisheries jetty development.
  • Maharashtra Coastal Economic Zone (MCEZ): Planned industrial development along Konkan coast.

Maharashtra Connection

JNPT is the gateway through which India's exports (textiles, pharmaceuticals, chemicals, auto components, electronics) enter global markets. The Atal Setu (MTHL, Jan 2024) is the most significant infrastructure addition — it cuts JNPT access time from 2+ hours to ~20 minutes. Navi Mumbai International Airport, once operational, will be among India's largest greenfield airports.

Exam Traps

  • Mumbai Port Authority Act 2021 replaced old Major Port Trusts Act 1963.
  • Maharashtra Maritime Board handles minor ports only (not JNPT or Mumbai Port).
  • Navi Mumbai International Airport is NOT yet operational (under construction as of 2024).
