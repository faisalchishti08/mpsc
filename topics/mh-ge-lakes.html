Lakes — Lonar, Pawna, Venna PREMAINSINTMH

Overview

Maharashtra's most significant lakes include the famous Lonar Crater Lake (a unique geological feature), Pawna Lake (Pune, man-made reservoir), Venna Lake (Mahabaleshwar, recreational), and several urban lakes and wetlands. Lonar is the globally exceptional one — a rare hyper-saline alkaline meteor impact crater lake — declared a Wildlife Sanctuary (2000) and Ramsar site (2020). MPSC tests Lonar's geological type, district, and Ramsar status.

Lonar Lake — Detailed Profile

  • Location: Lonar village, Buldhana district, Vidarbha.
  • Formation: Created by a meteorite impact approximately 52,000 years ago (some estimates: 570,000 years by other dating) — a matter of ongoing scientific debate; the impact crater is ~1.8 km in diameter.
  • Type: One of only four known hyper-saline alkaline impact craters with a lake in the world.
  • Water chemistry: Highly alkaline (pH ~10.5) and saline — both alkaliphilic bacteria and saltwater organisms thrive; no fish due to salinity.
  • Colour change (2020): Lonar Lake's water turned pink/flamingo pink in June 2020 — attributed to blooms of Halobacterium (halophilic archaea) and beta-carotene-rich algae (Dunaliella salina) — a scientifically notable and news-covered event.
  • Wildlife Sanctuary: Declared a WLS in 2000 (Lonar Wildlife Sanctuary).
  • Ramsar Site: Designated on 2 February 2020 (World Wetlands Day 2020).
  • Temple: An ancient Daitya Sudan Temple (to Vishnu, who killed the demon Lonasura here) on the rim; local legend explains the name "Lonar."

Powai Lake — Mumbai's Crocodile Habitat

  • Powai Lake (Mumbai Suburban) was built by the British in 1891 for Mumbai's water supply; now a recreational and ecological asset inside Mumbai.
  • The lake houses the Mugger crocodile (Crocodylus palustris) — a rare natural crocodile habitat inside a megacity. IIT Bombay campus is adjacent.

Venna Lake — Mahabaleshwar

  • Man-made lake (1942) on the Venna river, inside Mahabaleshwar hill station, Satara.
  • Named after the Venna river (one of the five rivers originating at Panchganga temple).
  • Famous for boating and horse riding; cold-water strawberry cultivation nearby.

Exam Traps

  • Lonar WLS declared 2000, Ramsar designation 2020 — do NOT mix the two years.
  • Lonar crater is ~52,000 years old — NOT "millions of years"; it is young by geological standards.
  • Vihar Lake is inside Sanjay Gandhi National Park (SGNP) — NOT in Lonavala or Pune; it is in Mumbai Suburban.
